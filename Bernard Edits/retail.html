Ka uomo, SF, CA (left) is an international leather clothing designer that needed to set up offices, storage and an outlet shop in an old warehouse on a limited budget. The solution was to create large rolling cages that could be used as both partitions and secure storage, allowing for the maximum flexibility for their business space planning

Jean Claude Jitrois, SF, CA (right) is a boutique for an urban upscale mall designed while Bernardin worked for Tecta Associates, Architects. The interior and all casework, fixtures, metal shelving and rack systems were custom designed for this unique retail store.
